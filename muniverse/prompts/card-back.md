# Card Back — Image Generation Prompts

Six design concepts for the universal back of all Muniverse cards. Generate each one and pick the best. All prompts target **2.5" × 3.5" portrait** (750×1050px at 300 DPI, or 1500×2100px for extra sharpness).

The design must be **perfectly symmetrical top-to-bottom** so the card looks the same regardless of orientation.

---

## 1 · Cosmic Portal

```
Design a trading card back, 5:7 portrait ratio, 1500×2100px.

A swirling cosmic vortex at the center of the card, pulling in streams of multicolored energy — deep purples, electric blues, molten golds, and emerald greens. Concentric rings of light radiate outward from the center. Tiny silhouettes of stars, planets, and abstract geometric shapes are caught mid-orbit around the vortex. At the exact center, a small glowing emblem reads "MUNIVERSE" in clean, futuristic sans-serif lettering.

The overall feel is deep space meets interdimensional gateway — mysterious, inviting, infinite.

The design must be vertically symmetrical (top half mirrors the bottom half) so the card looks identical in either orientation. Background is near-black at the edges, transitioning to intense luminous color near the center. No text other than the logo. No watermarks.
```

## 2 · Shattered Mirror / Kaleidoscope

```
Design a trading card back, 5:7 portrait ratio, 1500×2100px.

A kaleidoscope pattern radiating from a central "MUNIVERSE" emblem. The pattern is made of fractured glass or crystal shards, each shard containing a different abstract color palette — one warm and fiery, one cool and oceanic, one pastel and dreamy, one dark and stormy, one electric and neon. No specific characters or scenes visible — just color, light, and texture within each shard.

The central emblem is a stylized "M" inside a diamond shape, with "MUNIVERSE" in small elegant capitals beneath it. Fine gold or silver linework traces the edges of each shard. The overall palette is rich and jewel-toned against a deep charcoal background.

The design must be vertically symmetrical. A thin ornate border (2-3mm) frames the entire card. No watermarks, no other text.
```

## 3 · Constellation Map

```
Design a trading card back, 5:7 portrait ratio, 1500×2100px.

A deep midnight-navy celestial map with five custom constellations arranged in a pentagon around a central star. Each constellation is a stylized icon representing a stat category:
- Top: a glowing crystal (Magic & Mystery)
- Upper-right: a clenched fist with energy lines (Power & Action)
- Lower-right: a fox or jester mask (Mind & Mischief)
- Lower-left: an anatomical heart with a halo (Heart & Soul)
- Upper-left: a four-leaf clover with sparkles (Luck & Destiny)

The constellations are connected by thin golden line-art, like star chart illustrations. Tiny scattered stars fill the background. At the exact center, a larger star or compass rose bears the text "MUNIVERSE" in elegant serif capitals.

Subtle deep-purple nebula clouds drift behind the star field. The overall feel is astronomical, elegant, and timeless — like a page from a centuries-old star atlas. Thin gold border around the card edge.

Vertically symmetrical. No watermarks.
```

## 4 · Interlocking Gears / Clockwork

```
Design a trading card back, 5:7 portrait ratio, 1500×2100px.

An intricate mandala made entirely of interlocking gears, cogs, and clockwork mechanisms. The gears radiate from a central hub bearing the "MUNIVERSE" text in engraved metallic lettering. Larger gears at the center, smaller ones toward the edges, creating a sense of infinite mechanical complexity.

Color palette: bronze, antique gold, and tarnished silver metallic tones against a dark walnut-brown or deep burgundy background. The gears have fine engravings — tiny runes, star patterns, and abstract symbols etched into their surfaces.

The overall feel is steampunk-meets-sacred-geometry — every universe is a cog in one grand machine. Subtle ambient lighting as if the gears are lit by warm candlelight from within.

Vertically symmetrical. Thin decorative border with corner flourishes. No watermarks.
```

## 5 · Playing Card Classic (Reimagined)

```
Design a trading card back, 5:7 portrait ratio, 1500×2100px.

A classic playing card back pattern — a symmetrical, tiled, ornate repeating design — but reimagined with Muniverse motifs. The repeating pattern weaves together five small icons representing the stat categories: a crystal, a lightning bolt, a fox mask, a heart with wings, and a lucky star. These icons are arranged in an Art Deco or Art Nouveau repeating lattice.

Color palette: deep royal purple and midnight blue as the primary field, with the pattern rendered in metallic gold linework. A central cartouche contains the "MUNIVERSE" wordmark in gold serif capitals, surrounded by a decorative laurel or geometric frame.

The design should feel premium and tactile — like something you'd find on a luxury deck of cards. The pattern is dense but not cluttered. Perfectly symmetrical top-to-bottom and left-to-right.

Ornate double-line border with small diamond accents at the corners. No watermarks.
```

## 6 · Dimensional Tear

```
Design a trading card back, 5:7 portrait ratio, 1500×2100px.

A dark, richly textured surface — like aged leather or weathered parchment — covering the entire card. Down the exact center, a glowing vertical tear or crack splits the surface, revealing brilliant multicolored energy underneath: swirling galaxies, neon light streams, and prismatic fractals visible through the narrow opening.

The tear glows with soft light that illuminates the surrounding texture, creating a subtle halo effect. The "MUNIVERSE" wordmark is embossed into the dark surface above the tear, as if stamped into leather, with a faint inner glow.

The overall feel is minimal but dramatic — something ancient containing something infinite. The crack suggests stories from other worlds breaking through. Color contrast: dark warm browns and blacks for the surface, vivid rainbow energy for the tear.

Vertically symmetrical. Subtle worn border at the card edges. No watermarks.
```
