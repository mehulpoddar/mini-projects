# Card Back — Image Generation Prompt

## Shattered Mirror / Kaleidoscope

```
Design a trading card back, 5:7 portrait ratio, 1500×2100px.

A kaleidoscope pattern radiating from a central "MUNIVERSE" emblem. The pattern is made of fractured glass or crystal shards, each shard containing a different abstract color palette — one warm and fiery, one cool and oceanic, one pastel and dreamy, one dark and stormy, one electric and neon. No specific characters or scenes visible — just color, light, and texture within each shard.

The central emblem is a stylized "M" inside a diamond shape, with "MUNIVERSE" in small elegant capitals beneath it. Fine gold or silver linework traces the edges of each shard. The overall palette is rich and jewel-toned against a deep charcoal background.

The design must be vertically symmetrical. A thin ornate border (2-3mm) frames the entire card. No watermarks, no other text.
```
